# DataPlus - Learning Platform Guidance

1. Click on Login

If you have an account, click on Login. If not, Sign up to create an account.

You have the option to sign in with Google or sign up with email and password. You can also change the language if you don't understand English or French.

3. Click on Email

You can also log in (Sign in) with email and password.

# DataPlus - Learning Platform

If this is your first time, you can create an account.

5. Click on Student

If you are a student, click on the student option. If you are a teacher, click on the teacher option.

After selecting if you are a student or teacher, choose to sign in with Google or using email. Enter your details and click on create account.

This shows create account using Google. It works the same as the student option.
